ABOUT ME

Hey, this is Gab. I was born and raised in Rome and recently I graduted in Physics at Princeton University, where I also competed as a varsity athlete in the Track & Field team. In my time at Princeton, I developed a growing interest in cosmology, specifically in topics related to the origin and evolution of the universe, and had several opportunities to work alongside outstanding physicists in this field. The more I learned, the more I felt the need to share my knowledge and experience, both academic and not, with others. Thus, in my second year I began working, through the University, as a Tutor and Learning Consultant, specifically to help students develop learning strategies to thrive in STEM courses.
In the last 3 years I worked with more than 30 students from a variety of disciplines and I developed with each of them an individualized and strategic approach to learning that enables the student to make the most of lectures, precepts and readings; to manage their time and achieve their goals while maintaining a healthy balance.
Currently, I am seeking to mentor high school students with interest in a career in STEM and share my knowledge and passion for studies in math and physics through a lasting commitment to promoting success.
